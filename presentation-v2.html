The Business Model

Two Revenue Streams

1. Challenge Fees

~70%

of revenue

  • $99-199 per challenge attempt
  • ~75% failure rate (industry standard)
  • Users retry = recurring revenue

Example: 1,000 users × 2.5 attempts × $99 = $250K

2. Profit Share

~30%

of revenue

  • 20% of funded trader profits (they keep 80%)
  • ~25% of users reach funded status
  • Real P2P trading = ongoing share

Example: 250 funded × $500 profit × 20% = $25K/month

"Proven FTMO model: Challenge fees provide upfront revenue, profit share creates recurring income from successful traders."
