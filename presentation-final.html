How It Works: Challenge Rules

1.

Challenge Phase

●Duration: 30 days ●Profit Target: +15% ROI ●Max Daily Loss: -5% ●Max Total Loss: -10% ●Min Bets: 30 bets, 3+ sports ●Min Avg Odds: 1.50 ●Max Bet Size: 10% bankroll ●Fee: €99
2.

Verification Phase

●Duration: 60 days ●Profit Target: +10% ROI ●Max Daily Loss: -5% ●Max Total Loss: -10% ●Min Bets: 50 bets, 3+ sports ●Min Avg Odds: 1.50 ●Max Bet Size: 10% bankroll ●Fee: Included
3.

Funded Account

$10K-50K LEVL capital for Polymarket trading • 80/20 profit split • Monthly withdrawals • Same risk rules apply

Why these rules? Min avg odds (1.50) prevent arbitrage. Min bets (30/50) ensure statistical significance. 3+ sports diversification tests broad knowledge.

Risks & Mitigation

Financial Risks

  • Regulatory Compliance
    Sports betting laws vary by jurisdiction
  • Capital Liability
    Funded accounts create real liability exposure
  • Fraud & Abuse
    Coordinated betting, account manipulation

Operational Risks

  • Sports Data Reliability
    API downtime, incorrect odds, delayed updates
  • Platform Scalability
    High-volume trading during live events
  • Polymarket Dependency
    Reliance on Polymarket API availability and sports market coverage
Mitigation Strategy: Leverage FTMO's proven compliance framework, fraud detection systems, cloud infrastructure, and risk management expertise. Partner with tier-1 sports data providers for reliability.

Key Risks & When to Stop

Understanding what we can fix vs. what kills the project

🔄 Risks We'll Iterate On

  • ✓ Challenge calibration
    Adjust difficulty through iteration
  • ✓ User retention
    Improve UX, incentives, onboarding
  • ✓ Polymarket integration
    Optimize API usage, add fallbacks
  • ✓ Market liquidity
    Focus on high-volume markets first

🚨 Deal-Breaker Risks

  • ❌ Acquisition economics
    CAC exceeds LTV, no path to profitability
  • ❌ Regulatory blocker
    Prohibitive licensing in key markets
  • ❌ Polymarket shutdown
    Platform dependency risk materializes
  • ❌ Fraud at scale
    Cannot prevent systematic abuse
Philosophy: Iterate on product/UX issues. Stop on fundamental business model failures. Know the difference.
