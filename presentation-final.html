LEVL Prop Model

Users prove their sports prediction skills through structured challenges.
Successful participants get funded demo accounts to trade on P2P
markets. LEVL shares profits with skilled predictors.

Revenue Streams

Primary: Challenge Fees (~70%)

  • ● $99-199 per challenge attempt
  • ● ~75% fail rate (industry standard)
  • ● Users retry multiple times

Example: 1,000 users

1,000 × 2.5 attempts × $99

$247K

Secondary: Profit Share (~30%)

  • ● 20% of funded trader profits
  • ● ~25% reach funded status
  • ● Recurring monthly revenue

Example: 250 funded traders

250 × $500 profit × 20%

$25K/mo
Challenge fees cover costs, profit share = recurring revenue

4
