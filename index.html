How It Works: Challenge Rules

1.

Challenge Phase

●Duration: 30 days ●Profit Target: +15% ROI ●Max Daily Loss: -5% ●Max Total Loss: -10% ●Min Bets: 30 bets, 3+ sports ●Min Avg Probability: 50% ●Max Bet Size: 10% bankroll ●Fee: €99
2.

Verification Phase

●Duration: 60 days ●Profit Target: +10% ROI ●Max Daily Loss: -5% ●Max Total Loss: -10% ●Min Bets: 50 bets, 3+ sports ●Min Avg Probability: 50% ●Max Bet Size: 10% bankroll ●Fee: Included
3.

Funded Account

$10K-50K LEVL capital for Polymarket trading • 80/20 profit split • Monthly withdrawals • Same risk rules apply

Why these rules? Min avg odds (1.50) prevent arbitrage. Min bets (30/50) ensure statistical significance. 3+ sports diversification tests broad knowledge.

Key Risks & Mitigation

Critical risks and our approach to managing them

🎯 Market Competition

Existing competitors (PropMarket, FundedPoly) may improve products or acquire users faster

Mitigation: Sports-native differentiation, live-betting focus, superior UX, FTMO infrastructure advantage

💰 User Acquisition Cost

CAC may exceed targets if organic channels underperform or paid ads become expensive

Mitigation: Organic-first strategy, community building, influencer partnerships, referral program

📊 Challenge Pass Rate

Pass rate too high (>40%) breaks economics; too low (<10%) discourages users

Mitigation: Data-driven calibration during beta, continuous monitoring, rule adjustments based on results

🔗 Platform Dependency

Polymarket shutdown, API restrictions, or market liquidity issues could halt operations

Mitigation: Architecture designed for multi-platform support, fallback provider integration ready, direct partnership discussions, diversification strategy from Month 6

⚖️ Regulatory Changes

Sports betting laws may change, blocking access to key markets or requiring licensing

Mitigation: Demo capital model (not gambling), legal counsel from Q1, geographic filtering, proactive compliance

🎪 Product-Market Fit

Users may not value the challenge model or prefer traditional sportsbooks

Mitigation: Beta testing with 50-100 users, rapid iteration on feedback, clear go/no-go criteria

Risk Philosophy: Product issues (CAC, pass rate, PMF) are fixable through iteration. Platform dependency and regulatory risks require proactive mitigation and backup plans. Beta phase is our validation checkpoint.

Scenario A: Beta Doesn't Validate

What if metrics don't hit targets? Clear decision framework.

🚨 Failure Signals

KILL SIGNALS (Stop Immediately):

  • Pass rate >50% (too easy, economics broken)
  • Pass rate <10% (impossible, users give up)
  • Retry rate <20% (users don't believe)
  • NPS <0 (active dislike)
  • Major regulatory blocker
  • Systematic fraud we can't prevent

⚠️ PIVOT SIGNALS (Major Changes Needed):

  • Pass rate 35-45% (needs recalibration)
  • Retry rate 20-30% (lukewarm interest)
  • NPS 0-20 (works but not loved)
  • CAC >$50 (acquisition too expensive)

🔄 Pivot Options

If Challenge Calibration is Off:

Adjust profit targets (15% → 10% or 20%), change duration (30 → 45 or 20 days), modify risk limits

If Acquisition Too Expensive:

Pivot target market, change channels (organic vs paid), adjust pricing (€99 → €49 or €149), add referral incentives

If Product-Market Fit Weak:

Simplify onboarding, add gamification, improve UX on pain points, add educational content

If Infrastructure Can't Handle:

Scale back sports coverage temporarily, optimize Polymarket API, add caching, delay live betting

Decision Framework:

  • Week 4: First checkpoint - fix or continue?
  • Week 8: Final decision - go/pivot/stop?
  • Budget: €50K max for beta phase
  • Timeline: 60 days max, no extensions
