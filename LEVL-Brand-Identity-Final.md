# LEVL - Final Brand Identity

## BRAND ESSENTIALS

**Name:** LEVL
**Tagline:** "Level up your game"
**Positioning:** The skill-based sports prediction platform where you prove your edge, get funded, and trade on fair P2P markets.

---

## COLOR PALETTE - Electric Sport

### Primary Colors

**Electric Green** `#00FF88`
- RGB: `rgb(0, 255, 136)`
- Usage: Primary brand color, CTAs, success states, profit indicators, logo
- Symbolism: Winning, growth, "level up", sports energy

**Deep Black** `#0A0A0F`
- RGB: `rgb(10, 10, 15)`
- Usage: Backgrounds, primary UI surfaces
- Symbolism: Premium, modern, sophisticated

### Accent Colors

**Cyber Yellow** `#FBBF24`
- RGB: `rgb(251, 191, 36)`
- Usage: Highlights, achievements, premium features, logo accent
- Symbolism: Gold, victory, champions

**Electric Blue** `#1A6BFF`
- RGB: `rgb(26, 107, 255)`
- Usage: Links, secondary actions, informational elements
- Symbolism: Trust, technology, data

### Neutral Palette

**Light Grey** `#E5E5E5` - Primary text on dark
**Medium Grey** `#888888` - Secondary text
**Dark Grey** `#2A2A2F` - Borders, dividers

### Primary Gradient

**"Level Up" Gradient:**
```css
background: linear-gradient(135deg, #00FF88 0%, #FBBF24 100%);
```
- Usage: Logo, progress bars, hero sections, premium elements

---

## LOGO - Geometric L

### Concept
- **Icon:** Geometric "L" shape with stacked levels (4 layers)
- **Gradient:** Electric Green (bottom) → Cyber Yellow (top)
- **Wordmark:** "LEVL" in Montserrat ExtraBold
- **Symbolism:** Progression through levels, modern, gaming culture

### Logo Variations

**Horizontal (Primary):** `[L Icon] LEVL`
- Website headers, presentations, marketing

**Stacked:** Icon above "LEVL"
- Square spaces, app icon, social avatars

**Icon Only:** L symbol
- Favicon, small spaces, established brand contexts

**Wordmark Only:** "LEVL" text
- Minimal contexts, merchandise

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

**Display:** Montserrat Bold/ExtraBold/Black
- Logo, headlines, hero text

**Body:** Inter Regular/Medium/SemiBold/Bold
- Body copy, UI elements, navigation

**Monospace:** JetBrains Mono Regular/Bold
- Numbers, stats, odds, data displays

---

## BRAND VOICE

**Characteristics:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Modern, not trendy
- Clear, not jargon-heavy
- Energetic, not loud
- Inclusive, not exclusive

**Key Messages:**
1. "Prove your edge through structured challenges"
2. "Get funded. No own capital required."
3. "Peer-to-peer. No bookmaker margins."
4. "Level up through skill and consistency"
5. "Keep 80% of your profits"

---

## TARGET AUDIENCE

**Primary:** Analytical Alex (25-35, serious sports bettor, data-driven)
**Secondary:** Crypto Chris (22-32, early adopter, P2P enthusiast)
**Tertiary:** Social Sam (21-28, Gen Z, social bettor)

---

## COMPETITIVE POSITION

**Upper-left quadrant:** HIGH SKILL + LOW FEES

**Only platform combining:**
- FTMO challenge model
- P2P prediction markets
- Flashscore-like sports UX

**vs. Competitors:**
- Sportsbooks: No margins, no limits
- Exchanges: Funding opportunity, progression
- Prop Firms: P2P markets, modern UX
- FTMO: Sports vertical, separate brand
