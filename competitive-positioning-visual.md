## LEGEND & COMPETITORS

### ★ **LEVL (OUR POSITION)**
**Position:** Upper-left quadrant
- **High skill** (challenge-based evaluation)
- **Low/fair fees** (P2P markets, profit share model)

**Why this position:**
- Challenge model validates skill (like prop firms)
- P2P prediction markets = no bookmaker margin (like exchanges)
- Profit share instead of high entry barriers
- Premium positioning without premium pricing

---

### ◆ **FTMO (Trading Prop Firm)**
**Position:** Top-center
- **Very high skill** (strict challenge requirements)
- **Medium fees** (challenge fees, but fair profit share)

**Relation to LEVL:**
- Same parent company
- Similar business model (our inspiration)
- Different vertical (trading vs. sports)

---

### ● **Sports Prop Firms (BankrollU, MySportsFunding)**
**Position:** Upper-left to upper-center
- **High skill** (challenge-based)
- **Medium fees** (challenge fees)

**Difference from LEVL:**
- Traditional betting (not P2P prediction markets)
- Less tech-forward
- Smaller user base
- Less modern UX

---

### ○ **Betting Exchanges (Betfair, Smarkets)**
**Position:** Left-center
- **Medium skill** (understanding back/lay, market dynamics)
- **Low fees** (2-5% commission)

**Difference from LEVL:**
- No skill validation/challenge
- No funding model
- Mature, established players
- Desktop-heavy UX

---

### ■ **Traditional Sportsbooks (Bet365, Fortuna, Tipsport)**
**Position:** Right-bottom to right-center
- **Low to medium skill**
- **High fees** (built-in margins, often 5-10%+)

**Difference from LEVL:**
- Bet against the house
- Bookmaker sets odds
- High margins eat profit
- Account limits for winners

---

### △ **Fantasy Sports (DraftKings, FanDuel)**
**Position:** Left-bottom to center-bottom
- **Medium skill** (lineup construction, player knowledge)
- **Medium fees** (entry fees)

**Difference from LEVL:**
- Not real sports betting
- Different regulatory treatment
- More casual/entertainment focus

## DIFFERENTIATION SUMMARY

### **vs. Traditional Sportsbooks:**
- ✅ P2P markets (no bookmaker margin)
- ✅ Can't get limited for winning

### **vs. Betting Exchanges:**
- ✅ Funding opportunity (not just lower fees)

- ✅ P2P prediction markets (better pricing)
