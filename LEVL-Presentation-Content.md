**Visual: Revenue breakdown pie chart or bars**

**Revenue Streams:**

**1. Challenge Fees** (Primary - 70-75% of revenue)
- €99 per challenge attempt
- ~75% failure rate (industry standard)
- Recurring attempts (users retry)

**2. Profit Share** (Secondary - 20-25% of revenue)
- 20% of funded trader profits
- ~25% of users reach funded status
- Retention: Funded traders stay active

**3. Transaction Fees** (Future - 5% of revenue)
- 2% commission on P2P markets (post-MVP)
- Additional revenue from high-volume traders

| Profit share | 80-90% to trader | 80% to predictor |

- Proven model: FTMO generates $500M+ annual revenue

# SLIDE 15: RISK ANALYSIS

**Headline:** Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**Visual: Risk matrix or table**

**Financial Risks:**

🔴 **Challenge pass rate too high** (>40%)
- Impact: Revenue model breaks
- Mitigation: Dynamic difficulty adjustment, data-driven calibration, multiple tiers

🟡 **CAC inflation** (>$50)
- Impact: Unit economics deteriorate
- Mitigation: Organic-first strategy, referral program, community building

**Operational Risks:**

🔴 **P2P liquidity issues** (thin markets)
- Impact: Poor UX, users leave
- Mitigation: LEVL as market maker (initial), incentivize liquidity providers, start with high-volume markets

🟡 **Sports data reliability** (delays, errors)
- Impact: Settlement disputes, trust issues
- Mitigation: Multi-source verification, established providers (Opta, Stats Perform), manual override process

**Speaker Notes - Sports Data Costs:**
- FTMO has identified sports data providers for launch
- Estimated costs: €20-40K/month for tier-1 providers (Opta, Stats Perform) covering 3 sports
- Alternative lower-cost options available for MVP phase
- Data costs already factored into FTMO's infrastructure budget
- May be topic for discussion - be prepared to discuss provider selection and cost optimization strategies

**Regulatory Risks:**

🟡 **Gambling classification** (restrictive jurisdictions)
- Impact: Limited geographic reach
- Mitigation: Demo capital model (skill evaluation), clear ToS, avoid restricted states, legal counsel

🟢 **Market integrity** (manipulation)
- Impact: Fraud, reputational damage
- Mitigation: Volume limits, pattern detection, KYC/AML, leverage FTMO compliance

**Mitigation Summary:**
✅ Demo capital = regulatory safe zone
✅ FTMO infrastructure = compliance, fraud detection
✅ Data-driven optimization = economic balance
✅ Gradual rollout = learn & adapt

**Speaker Notes:**
- Proactive risk identification
- Mitigation strategies for each
- Demo capital model addresses biggest regulatory risk
- FTMO experience with compliance, fraud
- Iterative approach allows course correction
