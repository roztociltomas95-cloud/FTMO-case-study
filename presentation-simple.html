LEVL Prop Model

Users prove their sports prediction skills through structured challenges. Successful participants get funded demo accounts to trade on P2P markets. LEVL shares profits with skilled predictors.

How It Works

  1. Challenge Phase: User pays $99, gets demo capital, must hit profit target
  2. Verification: Second challenge validates consistency
  3. Funded Account: Access to $10K-50K demo capital for P2P markets
  4. Profit Share: User keeps 80%, LEVL takes 20%

Revenue Streams

Primary: Challenge Fees (~70%)

  • $99-199 per challenge attempt
  • ~75% fail rate (industry standard)
  • Users retry multiple times

Example: 1,000 users

1,000 × 2.5 attempts × $99

$247K

Secondary: Profit Share (~30%)

  • 20% of funded trader profits
  • ~25% reach funded status
  • Recurring monthly revenue

Example: 250 funded traders

250 × $500 profit × 20%

$25K/mo
FTMO-proven model: Challenge fees cover costs, profit share = recurring revenue
