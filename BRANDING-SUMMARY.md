## 📐 LOGO

**Geometric L Icon:**
- 4-8 stacked horizontal bars (depending on size)
- Vertical gradient: Green (bottom) → Yellow (top)
- Symbolizes level progression

**Variations:**
- Horizontal: `[L Icon] LEVL` (primary)
- Stacked: Icon above wordmark (square spaces)
- Icon only: For app icon, favicon
- Wordmark only: "LEVL" text
